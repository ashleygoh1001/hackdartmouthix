Teaching the right way.

Pedagogy ("peh-duh-gah-jee") is the method of teaching to structure lessons to best suit students' needs. This approach can vary based on factors such as subject, age of students, and goals. Some pedagogical models include small group discussion, peer interaction, inquiry-based learning, differentiated instruction, and project-based learning.

So what does this mean?

GoGoGogy uses these learning models and works with AI to build lesson plans based on the topic, number of students, and duration information you provide. We do the work to make the best possible lesson for you and your classroom!
